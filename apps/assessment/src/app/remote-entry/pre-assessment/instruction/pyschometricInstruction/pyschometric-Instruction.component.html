Simplifying Skills Skillsphere Assessment - Instructions

Welcome to the Simplifying Skills Skillsphere Assessment. Please read the following instructions carefully before you begin the test:

General Instructions:

  1. The assessment must be completed in one sitting. Once started, you cannot pause or restart the test.
  2. Ensure a stable internet connection before beginning the test to avoid interruptions.
  3. Use a desktop or laptop for the best experience. Avoid taking the test on a mobile device.
  4. Keep a pen and rough sheet handy for calculations in the aptitude section (if needed).
  5. Do not refresh or close the browser during the assessment, as this may lead to test submission.
  6. The test is time-bound, and each section must be completed within its allocated time. The system will automatically submit your answers when the time runs out.
  7. Once a question is answered and submitted, you cannot go back to change your response.

Section-wise Guide:

Aptitude Section (30 minutes, 45 questions)

  • Covers logical reasoning, numerical ability, and problem-solving skills.
  • Read each question carefully before selecting your answer.
  • Use rough Work only if required—no calculators or external help allowed.

Behavioral Section (20 minutes, 87 questions)

  • This section assesses personality traits, decision-making approach, and work ethics.
  • There are no right or wrong answers—respond honestly and consistently.
  • Choose the option that best reflects your thoughts, actions, and behavior in a given scenario.
