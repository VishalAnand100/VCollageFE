Simplifying Skills Skillsphere Assessment - Instructions

Section-wise Guide:

Behavioral Section (20 minutes, 87 questions)

  • This section helps understand your personality traits and behavioral patterns. There are no right or wrong answers—just be honest!
Sub-section 1: Interest & Preference:
  • This section explores your interests and career preferences.
  • Choose options based on what you genuinely enjoy, not what you think is expected.
Sub-section 2: Psychometric Assessment
  • This measures how you think, react, and process information.
  • Answer instinctively without overanalyzing.
Sub-section 3: Socio-Emotional Intelligence
  • Assesses your ability to understand and manage emotions.
  • Be truthful—this section helps identify your emotional strengths.
Sub-section 4: Adversity Quotient (AQ)
  • Tests how you handle challenges and setbacks.
  • There are no wrong answers; choose what best reflects you in difficult situations.
⏳ Remember: This section is time-bound, so don’t spend too long on any one question. Stay calm and answer naturally. If time runs out, your answers will be automatically submitted.
